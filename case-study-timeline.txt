Step 01 - Intake
User reports inability to access shared drive. Ticket opened, severity assessed.

Step 02 - Scope Check
One user or global? Confirmed isolated. Connectivity and account status verified.

Step 03 - Root Cause
Permission misconfiguration — user missing from the correct security group.

Step 04 - Resolution
Corrected ACL, refreshed session, re-tested end-to-end with the user.

Step 05 - Closure
User confirmed. Ticket documented with resolution notes and prevention steps.
